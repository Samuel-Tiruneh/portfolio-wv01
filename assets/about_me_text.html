About Me

Hey there! I'm Samuel Tiruneh, born and raised in Gondar, Ethiopia. I was born on April 10, 2001, from Azanu Malede and Tiruneh Cheklu. My educational journey began at Beza Primary School, and I later moved on to Fasiledes High School. I'm particularly interested in website development, and my long-term goal is to become a successful full-stack developer, creating cutting-edge solutions for clients.
